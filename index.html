About the Book

"Inside Out: A Guide to Biohacking Your Life by Understanding Your Gut Microbiota" is a groundbreaking guide that reveals how mastering your gut health can unlock peak mental and physical performance. Dr. Nikita Dinger combines cutting-edge scientific research with practical, actionable strategies to help you optimize your gut microbiome, enhancing everything from mental clarity and emotional resilience to energy levels and immunity. This book empowers you to take control of your health with personalized biohacking techniques, making sustainable, long-term improvements to your overall well-being.

About the Author

Dr. Nikita Dinger is an accomplished researcher and entrepreneur with a Ph.D. in Cancer Biology. With experience working alongside Nobel Prize-winning scientists in top international laboratories, she has become a leading expert in gut microbiome research and biohacking. As the founder of BioMeBar, a wellness company focused on AI-backed gut analysis, Dr. Dinger is dedicated to making scientific insights accessible and actionable for all, empowering people to take control of their health through gut biohacking. Currently based in California's Bay Area, she continues to push the boundaries of microbiome science to improve everyday health.
